Jacket Potato Recipe

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Tuna
  • Sweetcorn
  • Mayonnaise
  • Capers
  1. Bake Potatoes in oven for 40 minutes.
  2. Break down Tuna chunks into a pasty texture.
  3. Mix all the other ingredients except for Potatoes.
  4. Slice Potato open and pour in the other ingredients mixture.

Enjoy!
