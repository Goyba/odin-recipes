Chili Con Carne Recipe

Ingredients

  • Olive oil (tablespoon
  • Chopped onions
  • Minced meat (500g)
  • Black beans (100g)
  • Basmati rice (add to portion)
  • Chili Con Carne sauce (4 jars)
  • Peppers
  1. Fry onions until golden.
  2. Add chopped peppers.
  3. Add mince meat and cook until brown.
  4. Add black beans.
  5. When water is gone, add Chili Con Carne sauce.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Cook rice seperately per instructions on label.

Enjoy!
